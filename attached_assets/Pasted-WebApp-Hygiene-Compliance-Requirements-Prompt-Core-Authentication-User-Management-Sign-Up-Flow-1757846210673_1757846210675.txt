WebApp Hygiene & Compliance Requirements Prompt
Core Authentication & User Management
Sign-Up Flow
•	Email/Phone validation with verification codes
•	Password strength requirements (minimum 8 chars, mixed case, numbers, symbols)
•	Terms acceptance checkbox (required, cannot proceed without)
•	Privacy policy acknowledgment (required, cannot proceed without)
•	Duplicate account prevention (email/phone already exists handling)
•	Account activation flow via email/SMS verification
•	Onboarding completion tracking (profile setup, tutorial completion)
Sign-In Flow
•	Multi-factor authentication (MFA) option/requirement for sensitive accounts
•	"Remember me" functionality with secure session management
•	Forgot password flow with secure reset links (time-limited, single-use)
•	Account lockout protection after failed attempts with progressive delays
Account Management
•	Profile editing with real-time validation
•	Password change with current password verification
•	Email/phone change with verification of new contact method
•	Session management (view active sessions, remote logout)
Account Deletion
•	Account deletion request with confirmation steps
•	Data retention explanation (what gets deleted vs. anonymized)
•	Export data option before deletion
Legal & Compliance Pages
Privacy Policy (I have a privacy policy which I want to upload in the webapp)
•	Data collection explanation (what, why, how)
•	Data usage and processing purposes
•	User rights (access, rectification, erasure, portability)
•	Data retention periods and deletion policies
•	International data transfers and safeguards
•	Policy update notification process
Terms of Service/Usage (I have a terms of service which I want to upload in the webapp)
•	Service description and scope
•	User responsibilities and acceptable use
•	Prohibited activities and content restrictions
•	Intellectual property rights (yours and users')
•	Service availability and maintenance disclaimers
•	Limitation of liability and disclaimers
•	Termination conditions (user and platform initiated)
•	Dispute resolution and governing law
•	Modification notification process
•	Contact information for legal matters
Cookie Policy (GDPR Requirement)
•	Cookie types explanation (essential, functional, analytics, marketing)
•	Cookie consent management with granular controls
•	Third-party cookies disclosure (Google Analytics, marketing pixels)
•	Cookie duration and deletion information
•	Browser cookie management instructions
•	Consent withdrawal process
Security & Technical Hygiene
Security Headers & Configuration
•	HTTPS enforcement (SSL/TLS certificates)
•	Content Security Policy (CSP) headers
•	HSTS (HTTP Strict Transport Security) implementation
•	Rate limiting on APIs and forms
•	Input validation and sanitization
•	SQL injection prevention
•	XSS protection measures
Error Handling & User Experience
•	Custom error pages (404, 500, maintenance)
•	Graceful error messages (user-friendly, not technical)
•	Network connectivity handling (offline states)
•	Loading states and progress indicators
•	Form validation (real-time and on submit)
•	Success confirmations for user actions
•	Undo functionality for destructive actions
Accessibility & Usability
Accessibility Compliance (WCAG 2.1 AA)
•	Keyboard navigation support
•	Screen reader compatibility (ARIA labels, semantic HTML)
•	Color contrast compliance (4.5:1 ratio minimum)
•	Alt text for images and media
•	Focus indicators for interactive elements
•	Text scaling support (up to 200%)
•	Motion preference respect (reduced motion)
Mobile & Responsive Design
•	Mobile-first responsive design
•	Touch-friendly interface (44px minimum touch targets)
•	Viewport meta tag configuration
•	App-like experience (PWA considerations)
•	Offline functionality where applicable
Communication & Notifications
Email Communication System
•	Transactional emails (welcome, verification, receipts)
•	Notification preferences management
•	Email unsubscribe mechanism (one-click)
•	Email deliverability optimization
•	Bounce and complaint handling
In-App Notifications
•	Notification permission request flow
•	Notification types and frequency controls
•	Push notification opt-out mechanism
•	Email vs. push notification preferences
Customer Support
•	Contact methods (email)
•	Feedback collection mechanism
Performance & Monitoring
Performance Optimization
•	Page load speed optimization (< 3 seconds)
•	Image optimization and lazy loading
•	CDN implementation for static assets
•	Database query optimization
•	Caching strategies implementation
Analytics & Monitoring
•	User analytics (with privacy compliance)
•	Error tracking and reporting
•	Performance monitoring (uptime, response times)
•	User feedback collection tools
•	A/B testing infrastructure
Business & Financial Hygiene
Subscription & Billing (if applicable)
•	Transparent pricing display

•	Payment method management
•	Invoice generation and history
•	Refund policy and process
•	Subscription cancellation flow
•	Dunning management for failed payments
Internationalization (if global)
•	Multi-language support
•	Currency localization
•	Date/time format localization
•	Legal compliance per jurisdiction
•	Tax calculation and collection
Content & Community (if applicable)
Content Management
•	Content moderation policies and tools
