# HiPo AI Coach Design Guidelines

## Design Approach
**Reference-Based Approach**: Drawing inspiration from modern professional development platforms like LinkedIn Learning, Coursera, and mentorship apps, emphasizing clean professionalism with energetic accents suitable for Indian professionals seeking career growth.

## Core Design Elements

### A. Color Palette
**Primary Colors:**
- Royal Blue: 233 68% 59% (primary brand #4C62E3)
- Coral Accent: 3 76% 71% (accent #EE7D79)
- Pure White: 0 0% 100% (backgrounds light mode)
- Charcoal Gray: 220 15% 15% (backgrounds dark mode)

**Supporting Colors:**
- Light Gray: 210 10% 96% (card backgrounds, dividers)
- Medium Gray: 210 8% 50% (secondary text)
- Success Green: 142 71% 45% (achievements, progress)
- Warning Orange: 25 95% 53% (alerts, notifications)
- Error Red: 0 84% 60% (validation errors)

### B. Typography
**Display Font**: Montserrat (Google Fonts) - weights 500, 600, 700 for headings and titles
**Body Font**: Karla (Google Fonts) - weights 400, 500, 600 for all body text and UI elements
**Hierarchy**: Display sizes text-2xl to text-5xl, body sizes text-sm to text-xl

### C. Layout System
**Spacing Primitives**: Tailwind units 3, 4, 6, 8, 12, 16
- p-6 for card padding
- m-8 for section margins
- gap-6 for component spacing
- h-12 for standard button heights

### D. Component Library

**Navigation**: Clean header with vulture logo, mobile-first bottom tabs with coach access
**Coach Cards**: Rounded-xl cards with subtle shadows, diverse coach avatars, coral accent borders on hover
**Chat Interface**: Modern bubble design with Royal Blue user messages, light gray coach responses
**Progress Tracking**: Circular progress indicators with coral fills, milestone badges
**CTA Buttons**: Royal Blue primary buttons, coral accent secondary buttons, outline variants with blur backgrounds over images
**Forms**: Clean inputs with subtle borders, coral focus states

### E. Mobile-First Experience
**Hero Section**: Minimal hero with vulture logo, empowering headline, and immediate coach CTA
**Coach Selection**: Card grid showcasing diverse coach personas with specialties
**Dashboard**: Progress cards, saved insights, and quick coach access
**Conversation Flow**: Full-screen chat with coach branding and contextual suggestions

## Visual Treatments

**Gradients**: Subtle Royal Blue to lighter blue gradients for hero sections
**Backgrounds**: Clean whites with light gray accents, strategic coral highlights
**Shadows**: Soft elevated shadows (shadow-lg) for cards and floating elements
**Icons**: Heroicons for consistency, coral accent colors for active states
**Borders**: Minimal use, coral accent borders for emphasis

## Key Design Principles
1. **Aspirational Energy**: Royal Blue conveys trust and professionalism, coral adds warmth and motivation
2. **Inclusive Diversity**: Coach avatars and imagery represent India's diverse professional landscape  
3. **Empowerment Focus**: Clean, uncluttered design removes barriers to career growth access
4. **Professional Credibility**: Montserrat headings establish authority, Karla ensures readability
5. **Mobile Accessibility**: Touch-friendly interface prioritizing ease of use

## Images
**Vulture Logo**: Minimalist vulture silhouette positioned in header/navigation, representing aspiration and growth
**Coach Avatars**: Diverse professional illustrations representing different backgrounds, ages, and specialties - placed on coach cards and chat headers
**Empowerment Graphics**: Simple line illustrations showing career progression, networking, skill development - used sparingly in onboarding
**No Large Hero Image**: Clean, text-focused hero section with logo and clear value proposition, maintaining professional minimalism while ensuring immediate access to coaching features

The design balances professional credibility with approachable energy, using the Royal Blue-Coral combination to create trust while motivating career advancement for Indian professionals.
